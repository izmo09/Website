Call of Duty Mobile Multiplayer

Fast-Paced Multiplayer Gameplay

Call of Duty: Mobile Multiplayer delivers fast-paced first-person shooter action designed for competitive play. Players engage in intense matches that require quick reflexes, precise aiming, and strong teamwork.

Each match takes place on iconic maps inspired by classic Call of Duty titles, providing a familiar yet exciting experience for players.

Weapons and Loadouts

Multiplayer mode features a wide range of weapons including assault rifles, sniper rifles, SMGs, and shotguns. Players can customize their weapons using the Gunsmith system.

Attachments such as scopes, barrels, and magazines allow players to tailor their loadouts to fit different playstyles and game modes.

Multiplayer Game Modes

Call of Duty Mobile Multiplayer includes classic modes such as Team Deathmatch, Domination, Hardpoint, and Search and Destroy.

These modes focus on objective-based gameplay, map control, and team coordination, making multiplayer matches both challenging and rewarding.
